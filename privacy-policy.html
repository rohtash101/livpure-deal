Privacy Policy Of Liv Pure

At LivPure, we prioritize the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect your privacy. If you have any questions or need further information regarding our Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us.
Log Files

Like many websites, LivPure follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit the website, which is a common practice among hosting companies. The information collected by these log files includes details such as internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamps, referring/exit pages, and potentially the number of clicks. This data is used for analyzing trends, managing the site, tracking users’ movement on the website, and gathering demographic information. Please note that this data is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Cookies and Web Beacons

LivPure uses cookies, which are small files that store information such as visitor preferences and the pages of the website accessed. These cookies help to enhance the user experience by customizing the web content based on the visitor’s browser type and other information.

Additionally, third-party vendors, such as Google, may use cookies, including the DART cookie, to serve ads based on your visits to LivPure and other sites on the internet. This allows Google to display ads relevant to your interests.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

LivPure's Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party advertisers or other websites. These third-party vendors may use cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons in their ads and links that appear on LivPure, and these technologies are sent directly to your browser. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These third-party ad servers use this information to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the ads you see across the web.

Please be aware that LivPure has no access to or control over the cookies used by third-party advertisers.

For detailed information about third-party ad networks and their respective privacy practices, you can consult the Privacy Policies of these external parties. You can also opt-out of certain advertising practices, such as disabling cookies through your browser settings. For more information on cookie management in specific browsers, please refer to the browser's help section.

Children’s Privacy

Protecting the privacy of children is especially important to us. We encourage parents and guardians to monitor and guide their children’s online activities. LivPure does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that your child has provided such information on our website, please contact us immediately, and we will make every effort to remove that data from our records.

Online Privacy Policy Only

This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected through our online activities and pertains specifically to visitors of our website. It does not apply to information gathered offline or through channels other than this website.

Consent

By using our website, you consent to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy and agree to abide by our Terms and Conditions.

Updates to the Privacy Policy

We may update or change our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your personal information.
